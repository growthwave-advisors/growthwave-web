## Pipeline Stage Guide

### Understanding the JV Partners Pipeline

| Stage | Definition | What It Means | Action Items |
|-------|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| **Identified** | You met them at WOWCON, exchanged info | "Person + opportunity identified" | Send sequence, build relationship |
| **Relationship Building** | They're engaging (opened emails, booked call, replied) | "They're interested, building rapport" | Schedule & complete strategy call, understand their goals |
| **Qualified** | Call completed; they fit your JV criteria | "This is a real opportunity" | Present specific deal or partnership framework |
| **Deal Presented** | You've pitched a deal or opportunity | "They know what you can do together" | Negotiate terms, send agreements |
| **Committed** | They're in, paperwork signed or LOI executed | "Partner confirmed for deal(s)" | Execute deal, maintain relationship |

### When to Move Between Stages
- **Identified â†’ Relationship Building:** When they respond/engage or book a call
- **Relationship Building â†’ Qualified:** After strategy call if they meet your criteria
- **Qualified â†’ Deal Presented:** When you have a specific deal to offer them
- **Deal Presented â†’ Committed:** When they sign LOI or agreement

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Email bounced / invalid address**
- Check the email address against their LinkedIn or company website
- Ask via another channel (LinkedIn DM) for correct email
- Update in GHL

**Q: They replied asking about pricing/services**
- Don't sell in emailâ€”invite them to a call instead
- Use template: "Great question! Let's carve out 15 min to talk through what this could look like for your specific situation. [calendar link]"

**Q: They're interested but it's not the right time for them**
- Add to nurture track (monthly check-ins with helpful content)
- Tag as `engagement:nurture`
- Check in every 30 days

**Q: They unsubscribed**
- Respect it, remove from campaigns
- Don't re-add unless they specifically ask

**Q: No response to any emails**
- This is normal (typical open rate: 20-30%, click rate: 5-10%)
- Send one final gentle note around day 14
- Move to monthly nurture if no response
- Not everyone is a fit; that's okay
